Purdy’s Towing: Providing reliable towing services in Ketchikan for over 20 Years

Navigating tows in the terrain of Southeast Alaska for over two decades, Purdy’s Towing in Ketchikan is your trusted partner for dependable service in challenging environments, prioritizing safety, care for our community and its people, ready to assist you whenever you need a lift.
